Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HM Academy, Fatehpur UP — fees, batches, who can join and what you'll get.

What is HM Academy (Hilal Madany)? +
HM Academy (Hilal Madany) is Under registered society in Khalilnagar, Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh. Founded by Syed Hilaluddin Madany (14+ years MEP & FM experience), HM Academy's mission is to bridge the education gap between rural and urban India — providing engineering, management, AI, sales, procurement, entrepreneurship and academic skills from Class 5 onwards, aligned with India Vision 2035 and Make in India.
Can Class 5 students join HM Academy? +
Yes. HM Academy provides strong academic foundation coaching from Class 5 onwards in Maths and Science for UP Board, CBSE, and ICSE students. Using PhET simulations, AI tools and GeoGebra, we make STEM exciting and accessible for all students — regardless of grades or background. In-person in Fatehpur area and online across India.
Do I need an MBA to become an entrepreneur through HM Academy? +
No MBA needed. HM Academy's entrepreneurship programme teaches you business model building, sales, marketing, digital go-to-market, procurement, project management and international scaling — all practical skills that let you start and grow a business from any age. This is the alternative to an MBA, built for India's Vision 2035.
What is the fee for HVAC course at HM Academy in Fatehpur? +
HVAC course at HM Academy: ₹2,000 for the 6-week programme, up to ₹8,000 for the full 6-month ASHRAE-standard course with certificate. Weekend and weekday batches available. The complete MEP + Entrepreneur flagship programme is ₹12,000–₹18,000 (EMI available). Academic coaching starts from ₹300/month.
Can non-technical people learn procurement and site engineering? +
Absolutely. HM Academy's Procurement, Site & Maintenance Engineering programme is specifically designed so that non-technical people can master vendor management, procurement strategy, site supervision and maintenance planning. Many HM Academy students with no technical background go on to start their own contracting businesses.
Is HM Academy online or only in Fatehpur? +
Both. In-person classes are held in Khalilnagar, Fatehpur UP (near Haswa and Chak Korra Sadat). Online batches are open to students and professionals across all of India — including Kanpur, Allahabad, Prayagraj, Lucknow and beyond. Weekend batches are specifically for working professionals.
How does HM Academy support the Make in India and India Vision 2035 mission? +
Every programme at HM Academy is built around India's 2035 self-reliance goal. We train individuals in engineering, management, AI, digital, procurement and entrepreneurship skills so India produces world-class talent from rural Uttar Pradesh — contributing to Make in India, Digital India, Startup India and Atmanirbhar Bharat from Day One of training.
